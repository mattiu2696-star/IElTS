4 Cấu Trúc Câu Cơ Bản

① S + V + adv + time
The income of RB increased dramatically from 2005 to 2010.
② There was + adj + NOUN + in S + time
There was a dramatic increase in the income of RB from 2005 to 2010.
③ The period + saw/witnessed + Noun Phrase
The period 2005–2010 witnessed a dramatic increase in RB's income.
④ NOUN + was seen/witnessed + in S + time (Passive)
A dramatic increase was seen in the income of RB from 2005 to 2010.

Câu Nâng Cao – Band 7+

Chung chủ (S1=S2):
After VING1, S V2
Having VPII, S V2
Despite VING1, S V2
S V1, before VING2
Khác chủ (S1≠S2):
S1 V1, after which S2 V2
S1 V1, followed by NOUN PHRASE
S1 V1, compared with/opposed to NOUN

Trích Dẫn Số Liệu

from…to · at · by (verb) / of (noun) · between…and

📊 Module B – Graphs WITHOUT TREND · So Sánh
🔍 Nhận biết: Chỉ có 0 hoặc 1 mốc thời gian – tập trung so sánh giữa các nhóm/đối tượng.

1. Superlative (Cao/Thấp nhất)

The highest percentage in [group] belongs to [X].
[X] accounts for the highest percentage.
The highest % of time is spent on [X] in [group].
[Group] spend the highest % of their time [V-ing].

2. Comparative – Cơ Bản

The % of [X] is higher than that of/on [Y].
A higher % of time is spent on [X] than on [Y].
More time is spent on [X] than on [Y].

3. Comparative – Nâng Cao (Với Mức Độ)

GENERAL: far/much >< slightly/minimally
DETAILED: figure + unit (12% / by 12%)
TIMES: twice/three times higher than

The % of [X] is much/twice/12% higher than on [Y].
A much higher % of time is spent on [X] than on [Y].

4. Verb Phrases Đặc Biệt

outnumber somebody by something
→ Women outnumber men by four to one.

surpass B by ± chênh lệch
→ [X] surpassed [Y] by 12%.

be superior to = greater in number than
→ The % of [X] is superior to that on [Y].

5. Equal Comparison

Degree: nearly/almost / exactly/precisely / twice/half

A, B, C recorded nearly equal/equivalent figures.
[X] holds the first/second place in [group].
The first position is taken/occupied/held by A.

6. Ranking & Connector

A comes top of the list, followed by B and C.
While S1 V1, S2 V2.
S1 V1 opposed to / compared with figure for S2.
In comparison with S2, S1 V1.
⚙️ Module C – Process · Quy Trình
🔍 Nhận biết: Human: industry, agriculture, construction, services. Nature: life cycle (animals), natural resources, weather.

Ngữ Pháp Cốt Lõi

Tense: Present Simple
Voice: Passive (S + BE + V-PII)

ACTIVE: People pick tomatoes.
PASSIVE: Tomatoes are picked and put in baskets.

Connector Trình Tự

Chức năngConnector
Bắt đầuFirst / Initially / To begin with
Tiếp theoThen / Next / After that / Following this
Sau đóSubsequently / The following step involves
Song songSimultaneously / Meanwhile / At the same time
Lựa chọnAlternatively
Kết thúcFinally / Ultimately / Once this stage is complete

Diễn Đạt Mục Đích

for + NOUN: for flavor enhancement
to / in order to + VERB: to ensure quality
so that + S+V: so that it can be sold
with a view to + VING: with a view to improving

Từ Vựng Life Cycle

Breeding: lay/deposit eggs · hatch · emerge · spawn · breed
Development: develop = grow up · feed on = consume · enlarge · mature = reach adulthood · feed on
Movement: migrate = move = travel · live in = occupy
Kết thúc: Having completed all these steps, the life cycle begins again.

Bước Tiếp Cận (How to Approach)

  1. Analyze: WHAT – WHERE – WHEN
  2. Skim: Title · số stages · starting & ending points · footnotes
  3. Outline: Ghi verbs (passive) + extra details ra nháp
  4. Write: Non-stop, dùng passive + connectors

Extra Details (Band 7+)

with + NOUN: with brand name, ingredients, and expiry date
which/where + relative clause: where ketchup can be purchased
2–4 stages thêm extra details là đủ cho Band 7
🗺️ Module D – Map · Bản Đồ (MỚI)
🔍 Nhận biết: Bản đồ thường xuất hiện cùng nhau (before/after) hoặc có thể kết hợp với Process. Mô tả sự thay đổi địa lý hoặc vị trí.

Từ Vựng Vị Trí (Spatial Vocab)

to the north/south/east/west of in the north/centre/south adjacent to next to / beside opposite between X and Y along the river/road on the outskirts of in the vicinity of surrounded by in the far north/corner

Từ Vựng Thay Đổi (Change Vocab)

Loại thay đổiCụm từ
Xây mớiwas built / was constructed / was developed / was added
Phá/dỡ bỏwas demolished / was knocked down / was removed / disappeared
Thay thếwas replaced by / gave way to / was converted into
Mở rộngwas extended / was expanded / was widened
Đổi tên/dùngwas renamed / was transformed into / was turned into
Không đổiremained / stayed the same / was retained / was unchanged

Thì Động Từ Cho Map

Past Simple: mô tả trong quá khứ
→ A hospital was built in 1990.

Present Perfect (Passive): thay đổi đến hiện tại
→ The forest has been cleared.

Used to be: trước đây là...
→ The area used to be farmland.

Now/Currently: hiện tại
→ The site is now a shopping mall.

Cấu Trúc Câu Map – Band 7+

Where once there was [X], there is now [Y].
[X], which used to be [old], has been converted into [new].
The [area] was transformed from [A] into [B].
[Building] was replaced by [new building] on the [north] side.
The road was extended to connect [A] with [B].

Bước Tiếp Cận Map

  1. Overview: Tổng kết thay đổi lớn nhất (mở rộng? phát triển? thu hẹp?)
  2. Nhóm theo vị trí: North → South → East → West, hoặc Center → Outskirts
  3. Nhóm theo loại thay đổi: Added / Removed / Retained
  4. Dùng cả 2 thì: past simple + present perfect

🧱 PEEL Structure – Cách Viết Body Paragraph Band 7+
💡 PEEL = Point → Explanation → Example → Link · Mỗi body paragraph nên đầy đủ 4 phần này để đạt Band 7+. Viết 4–6 câu/paragraph.
P – Point
Topic sentence – phát biểu ý chính của paragraph
One clear, direct sentence answering the essay question.
→ "Working for a single organization throughout one's career offers significant advantages in terms of career stability."
E – Explanation
Giải thích tại sao/như thế nào – 1-2 câu
→ "This is because long-term employees tend to develop deep institutional knowledge and are more likely to receive promotions and salary increases, leading to greater financial security."
E – Example
Ví dụ cụ thể – 1-2 câu (thực tế / giả thuyết)
→ "For example, in Japan, the tradition of lifelong employment has been linked to low staff turnover and high levels of employee loyalty, which in turn contributes to corporate stability."
L – Link
Kết nối về câu hỏi đề – 1 câu
→ "Therefore, it is clear that long-term employment can provide employees with a more secure and rewarding professional life."
